Launch

Launch is a 2 player competition style game we made at the end of our first year of gamedevelopment. The game is losely based on the space race between the Soviet Union and the United States of America. We interpreted this as running around a maze as two astronauts and finding all the parts stuffing them into your rocket and going to the one place not corrupted by capitalism SPAAACE!!!

On the programming side I was responsible for the game logic: the win state, the parts spawning and collecting as well as implementing the art.
