About Me

I am a game development student at MediaCollege Amsterdam.

My classmates describe me as charismatic & consistent when it comes to my work. I am proficient at JavaScript & Unity/C# and wish to learn C++ and Unreal Engine. The best way I can describe my skill set at this moment in time is all-round game programmer. The thing I enjoy and want to learn the most is making and developing gameplay mechanics like the dodge mechanic in my ‘LastSpells Project’ and the part collecting in the ‘Launch Project’. I enjoy working in a team as not only does it give me the ability help other people, it also gives me the extra motivation to get up and go beyond.

In the last couple of projects I participated in I have done a majority of the game logic in the launch project and the tower turning and thus the progression of the player in the ‘Babelen Project’, both of which can be found in the projects section. There are other things I want to get good at, such as basic art and level design for the purpose of

  1. Being able to make prototypes on my own, and
  2. Being a better asset to the dev team if/when the programming is done or at least has ground to a hold.

On the programming side I was responsible for the game logic: the win state, the parts spawning and collecting as well as implementing the art.
